CATravel

Students learn in a wide variety of ways. No matter their learning methods, all students have one thing in common: They will learn by doing something memorable.
At CAT, we want to inspire our students through travel. Traveling can illuminate the multiple perspectives of a place's history, awakens the senses and mind, as well as connects teachers and students to cultures, languages, and people through authentic learning experiences.
Every year CAT offers educational trips to different parts of the world in order to extend the learning experience outside of the classroom.
